# TwistYou Privacy Policy
Privacy Policy – TwistYou

Last updated: 16/11/2025

TwistYou (“the Application”) is developed by TwistYou (“we”, “us”).
This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the Application.

## 1. Information We Collect

TwistYou does not directly collect, store, or share any personal data from users.
We do not request your name, email, location, photos, or any sensitive information within the application.

## 2. Device Data Usage

The Application does not collect device information such as contacts, location, files, or hardware identifiers.

## 3. Advertising (AdMob)

TwistYou displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob.
AdMob may use anonymous identifiers to serve personalized or non-personalized ads.
For more information, you can review Google’s Privacy Policy:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

## 4. Minors

TwistYou is intended only for users who are of legal drinking age in their country.
It is not designed for individuals under 18 years old (or the applicable legal age).

## 5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy at any time. The latest version will always be available alongside the Application.
